About Dalgona Coffee

Dalgona Coffee is a south Korean dish which went viral during the covid -19 lockdown. The dish caan be made from a few recipes which are in your kitchen cupboard. What are you waiting for? Let's start!

Ingredients:

  • Instant Coffee Powder/Expresso Granules
  • Hot Water
  • Sugar
  • Hot Milk

Procedure

Step 1: Add coffee powder and sugar to hot water
Add 2 tbs of coffee powder to 1 cup of hot water. Add 2 tbs of sugar to the mixture.
Step:2 Stir well
Use a handheld mixer and stir the mixture well until it becomes creamy and fluffy.
Step:3 Serve it with milk
Add a cup of hot milk to the fluffy and creamy mixture. Serve it with a spoon and a straw. Now stir, drink and repeat to enjoy your homemade Dalgona Coffee.
